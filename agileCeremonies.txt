1. sprint planning -> 
      1. first agile ceremony(at starting of sprint)
      2. here team plans the work they will complete in upcoming sprint.

2. daily standup / daily meet ->
      1. everyday ~15 min.
      2. disscussion around -> 
                  what team did yesterday?
                  what they will be donig today?
                  is there any challenges? if yes then discussing solutions.

3. sprint review 
      1. at end of each sprint
      2. demonstration of work by each team member
      3. feedback is gathered from client and any improovement they
         want in upcoming sprint.

4. sprint retrospective
      1. team reviews what went well , what didn't 
      2. plans for improovement in next sprint.
